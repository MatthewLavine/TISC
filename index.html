💡 The Big Idea

The ALU (Arithmetic Logic Unit) is the CPU's calculator. Every math and logic operation flows through it:

After every ALU operation, flags update automatically: Zero (result = 0), Negative (top bit set), Carry (overflow). These flags will power decisions and loops in later iterations.
